    Measure of concentration Definition
    Molarity is the amount of a substance in a certain volume of solution. Molarity is defined as the moles of a solute per liters of a solution
    Normality is gram equivalent weight of solute per liter of solution. N = M x n. Where M is molarity, and n is the number of equivalents
